About me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio!
My name is Tereza Bělohoubková and I'm a design thinker.
My journey began in the world of science and technology and evolved into a passion for user experience.
During my studies I've developed a deep understanding of scientific principles, analytical thinking and research methods. These foundational skills have shaped my approach to solving problems.
Making a career shift wasn't easy, but when I set my mind to it, I knew working in a creative environment was my calling.
The cherry on top is providing users with design solutions that are intuitive and enjoyable to experience.
